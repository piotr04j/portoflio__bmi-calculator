BMI calculator

NOTE: BMI is only indicator of value fat in body. It isn't professional diagnostic tool.

  • Very severely underweight: to 15
  • Severely underweight: from 15 to 16
  • Underweight: from 16 to 18.5
  • Normal (healthy weight): from 18.5 to 25
  • Overweight: from 25 to 30
  • Obese Class I (Moderately obese): from 30 to 35
  • Obese Class II (Severely obese): from 35 to 40
  • Obese Class III (Very severely obese): from 40

The body mass index calculator gives everyone the opportunity to quickly calculate own body fat index. A high value of BMI can be an indicator of high body fatness. If you want to calculate your BMI, choose your sex Female or Male and enter your height in centimetres and weight in kilograms and click calculate.
